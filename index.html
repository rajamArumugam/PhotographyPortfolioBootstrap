About me

I'm a simple, fun-loving person who has a deep love for photography. Nature has a calming effect on me, and my photographic journey began with landscape photography. Photography ignites a strong passion within me, especially when I'm immersed in the beauty of nature. Autumn, my favorite season, serves as a great source of inspiration.
After the arrival of my newborn, my photography interests evolved to include portrait photography. I find immense joy in capturing authentic emotions in portraits. Ultimately, the ability to freeze moments in time and preserve cherished memories is what makes photography truly fulfilling.
